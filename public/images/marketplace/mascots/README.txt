MARKETPLACE MASCOT IMAGES FOLDER
=================================

This folder contains images for the 3D mascot templates displayed in the marketplace.

NAMING CONVENTION:
------------------
Use lowercase with hyphens, matching the template IDs for consistency:

1. maya-professional.png
2. zara-blob-companion.png
3. geometric-navigator.png
4. rex-the-retriever.png
5. phoenix-the-firebird.png
6. pip-the-penguin.png
7. orion-mech-unit.png
8. flux-abstract-form.png

ALTERNATIVE NAMING (if you prefer):
------------------------------------
You could also use the exact template IDs from the code:
- template-1.png (Maya Professional)
- template-2.png (Zara Blob Companion)
- template-3.png (Geometric Navigator)
- template-4.png (Rex the Retriever)
- template-5.png (Phoenix the Firebird)
- template-6.png (Pip the Penguin)
- template-7.png (Orion Mech Unit)
- template-8.png (Flux Abstract Form)

RECOMMENDED: Use the descriptive names (first option) as they are more maintainable.

IMAGE SPECIFICATIONS:
---------------------
- Format: PNG (or JPG/WEBP for better compression)
- Recommended size: 400x400px or 600x600px (square aspect ratio)
- Background: Transparent or matching the brand colors from the templates
